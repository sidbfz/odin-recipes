Burger

Description

This classic beef burger is juicy, flavorful, and grilled to perfection. Customize it with your favorite toppings for a delicious homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Your choice of toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Place the patties on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a bun and adding your desired toppings.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
