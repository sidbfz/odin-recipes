Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender pasta, all topped with a golden layer of melted mozzarella. It's the perfect comfort food for a family dinner or special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned. Drain the fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the beef mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer 4 lasagna noodles on top.
  7. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese, and 1/3 of the meat sauce. Repeat these layers twice more.
  8. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top layer of meat sauce.
  9. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
