Brownie

Description

These fudgy brownies are rich, chocolatey, and irresistibly moist. Perfect for chocolate lovers, they are easy to make and are always a hit at any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
  2. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Beat in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder until the batter is smooth.
  4. Spread the batter evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Do not overcook.
  6. Let the brownies cool in the pan before cutting into squares and serving.
